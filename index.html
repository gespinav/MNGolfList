🔒 Your Data & Privacy

Minnesota Public Golf stores all personal data on your device only. We collect no personal information and never share or sell your data.

✅Courses played — stored on your device only
✅Your round history — stored on your device only
✅Your course photos — stored on your device only
❌We do not collect your name, email, or location
❌We do not sell your data to anyone, ever
❌We do not use your rounds for advertising or profiling
❌We do not run third-party trackers or analytics
⛳ Handicap Index

Your USGA Handicap Index is stored only on this device and used to calculate course handicap and net scores alongside your rounds.

Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will also remove all stored data. Use the export options above to back up your rounds before clearing.
